Misty River Equestrian Center

Misty River Equestrian Center is located in Independence, MO and offers full care stalls, partial self-care stalls, private turnout dry lots, and pasture care.

They have fifty four stalls in two insulated barns with an outdoor arena and two indoor arenas.

Silvertooth Stables

Silvertooth Stables is located in Kansas City, MO and only offers full care stalls.

They have a newly restored climate controlled barn with an outdoor arena, indoor arena, and groomed riding trails.
